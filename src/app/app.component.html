About

Grocery Calculator is used to estimate the grocery items in your home, how many days that it will be last long...!!!

How to use

step 1:Select the Grocery item from the dropdown.

step 2:Click on the "Get Metrics" button to get the metrics of your selected grocery item. After clicing the button, you can able to see the respective metrics of the selected grocery item in the followed field.

step 3:Then in the next field, Enter the quantity that how much you were having of your selected item according to the displayed metrics.

step 4:Then in the next field, Enter how many members were in your home.

step 5:Then click on the calculate button to get the estimation days for your selected grocery item...!!
